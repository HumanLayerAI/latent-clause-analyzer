# Latent Clause Analyzer

> AI-powered contract analysis that reveals negotiation leverage, not just risk.
>
> ## What It Does
>
> Most contract analyzers flag risk. This one reveals **negotiation leverage** — showing not just what's weak, but how each party *perceives* that weakness, and what tactical move to make next.
>
> ## Features
>
> - **Dual Perspective Analysis** — See how each clause reads to you vs. the counterparty
> - - **Psychological Tactics Exposed** — Anchoring, loss aversion, framing effects decoded
>   - - **Actionable Negotiation Playbook** — Specific tactics for each flagged clause
>     - - **Smart Contract Detection** — Auto-detects MSA, NDA, or Employment contracts
>       - - **Traffic Light System** — Red/Yellow/Green severity ratings
>         -
>         - ## The Law × Psychology × AI Approach
>         -
>         - This tool applies behavioral economics principles to contract analysis:
>         - - Loss Aversion (Kahneman & Tversky)
>           - - Anchoring Bias
>             - - Framing Effects
>               - - False Equivalence Detection
>                 - - Complexity as Camouflage
